PropertiKu — Version 1.0 Release Notes (English)
================================================

--- SHORT (Play Store / "What's new") ---

PropertiKu 1.0 — first release.

Manage rental properties in one app: units & tenants, leases, bills & expenses, due reminders, cash flow, and reports (yield, projection, occupancy, mortgage calculator, charts). Simple or Pro mode. Indonesian & English. Backup your data (export/import). Light & dark theme. PWA — add to home screen. Data is stored on your device (local). Ideal for boarding houses, shophouses, and other rental portfolios.

--- FULL FEATURE LIST ---

CORE
- Rental property management as a web app / PWA; main data stored locally on the device (browser storage).
- Two UI modes: Simple (financial summary) and Pro (full analytics & reports).
- Languages: Indonesian & English (ID / EN toggle).
- Theme: light & dark.
- PWA: install / add to home screen, themed icons (teal/green).
- First-run onboarding.

MAIN NAVIGATION (bottom bar)
- Home — dashboard overview.
- Tenants — list & search tenants.
- Pay — all payment & expense records.
- Units — units by property, filters for occupied / vacant.
- Reports / Summary — label follows mode (full Reports vs Summary).

DASHBOARD (HOME)
- Greeting & quick stats: unit count, occupancy, overdue count.
- Business calendar — reminders for: lease end dates, property tax (PBB) if configured, permit expiry, open maintenance tickets.
- This month's cash flow — income, expenses, net profit.
- Upcoming dues — rent bills that are pending or overdue (sorted by due date).
- My properties — snapshot per property with occupancy.
- ROI per property (Pro) — quick indicators per property.

TENANTS
- Add / edit tenants, link to units & leases (start–end dates).
- Tenant archive & unit-level history.
- Search.

PAYMENTS (PAY)
- Filters: All / Unpaid / Paid / Overdue.
- Views: By date (timeline) or By tenant (accordion).
- Record rent income & expenses; mark paid, due dates, billing periods.

UNITS
- Filters: all / occupied / vacant.
- Add units (name, property, type: boarding, apartment, house, shophouse, office, etc.), rent, status, subtype / facilities.
- Bulk add multiple units per property (Pro).
- Unit photos, tenant history, per-unit settings.

REPORTS (PRO MODE)
- Overview — monthly/yearly P&L, yield vs purchase price, transactions, history.
- Yield — property comparison, gross/net yield, payback, annual costs, loan installments if set.
- Projection — assumptions for price & rent growth, horizon, per-property table.
- Risk simulation — vacancy, rent drop, expense increase, installment stress.
- Occupancy — bars per property, 6-month trend, leases ending soon.
- By property — cards: occupancy, income/net, yield, payback, potential rent.
- Mortgage (KPR) — payment simulator, closing costs, insurance, amortization, rent vs mortgage comparison, save installment to property.
- Charts — financial / occupancy visuals.
- Operations (Insight) — combined reminders, repair tickets, churn, rent change history.

FLOATING ACTION BUTTON (FAB)
- Add unit, add tenant, record payment, record expense, maintenance ticket (Pro).

SETTINGS
- UI mode: Simple / Pro.
- Light / dark theme.
- Export / import data (JSON backup).
- Telegram (optional) — reminders if configured.
- Property data — purchase price, PBB, annual costs, mortgage fields, permits, etc.

PRIVACY
- No mandatory cloud; data primarily stays on device.
- Export/import to move devices or keep backups.
